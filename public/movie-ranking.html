The Best Movies According to Dennis

My top 3 Movies of all-time.

Avengers: Endgame

The ending of a great Marvel Saga. So much action with comedy.
It is a love letter to comics collectors and marvel fans.

Forest Gump

A truly inspirational movie.
Won 1995 Best Picture at Oscar.

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

Great middle-earth themed movie that is adopted from the books of John Ronald Reuel Tolkien.
It sets a baseline of following movies and series with the same genre.
